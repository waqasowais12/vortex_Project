About Us

Vortex FX,provider of Forex CFDs, serves to bridge the divide between retail and institutional traders, thereby enhancing the accessibility of trading for all.
Start Trading

Vortex FX stands out as a leading provider of Forex CFDs, catering to a wide range of traders, from active day traders and scalpers to newcomers in the forex market. With state-of-the-art trading platforms, low latency connectivity, and top-tier liquidity, Vortex FX is revolutionizing online forex trading, granting traders access to pricing that was previously exclusive to investment banks and high net worth individuals.

Our management team boasts extensive experience in the Forex, CFD, and Equity markets across Asia, Europe, and North America, enabling us to meticulously select cutting-edge technology solutions and premium pricing providers in the market.

  • When was Vortex FX Founded?

    Vortex FX was founded by a group of seasoned professionals in the financial services sector, who played a pivotal role in the achievements of one of the largest CFD providers.

  • Why was Vortex FX Founded?

    Vortex FX aimed to narrow the divide between retail and institutional clients by introducing a trading solution that was traditionally exclusive to investment banks and high net worth individuals. This initiative led to the development of Vortex FX Raw Spread Connectivity, which delivers superior execution technology, reduced spreads, and unmatched liquidity. Committed to innovation and continuous enhancement, Vortex FX is dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technology that was previously inaccessible in the market.

  • Mission Statement

    Vortex FX's mission is to establish the finest and most transparent trading environment for both retail and institutional clients, enabling traders to concentrate more on their trading activities. Developed by traders for traders, Vortex FX is committed to providing exceptional spreads, execution, and service.

  • Regulatory Compliance

    Vortex FX recognizes the significance of adhering to all applicable laws, rules, regulations, policies, and standards. We are dedicated to maintaining rigorous management discipline and implementing top-notch controls within our compliance framework. Vortex FX is obligated to adhere to stringent financial standards, encompassing capital adequacy and audit requisites. We uphold client funds in segregated trust accounts with leading banking institutions, ensuring compliance with Client Money Handling regulations. These funds are kept separate from Vortex FX company funds and are not utilized for operational expenses or purposes.

  • Audit

    Vortex FX has enlisted the services of an independent, external auditor to complement our operational procedures and verify regulatory compliance.

  • Confidentiality

    Vortex FX implements stringent controls to oversee the transfer of confidential and sensitive client information both internally and externally, strictly on a need-to-know basis. We do not disclose client information unless explicitly requested by the client or mandated by law.
