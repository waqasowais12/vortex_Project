Raw Trader Plus

Earn rebates on your trading commissions and unlock a variety of exclusive benefits with each trade. Vortex FX's meticulously curated range of products, platforms, and account types provides access to the most popular and liquid markets worldwide, ensuring optimal trading opportunities.

Competitive Pricing

Vortex Markets' Raw Trader Plus is tailored for high-volume traders seeking competitive pricing and additional benefits. As a Raw Trader Plus member, you'll receive rebates on your trading commissions, refunded automatically and daily once specific criteria are met. Your rebate will vary based on the number of standard lots you trade—the more you trade, the higher your rebate.

Exclusive rebates for high-volume traders

Raw Trader Plus members receive a rebate on their trading commissions upon meeting the trading volume requirements. Rebates are calculated based on the number of trades placed and closed. To maintain Raw Trader Plus membership, you must consistently meet the volume requirements over a 3-month period. Please refer to the terms and conditions for this program for more information.
