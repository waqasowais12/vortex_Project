    • What is Equinix NY4 and LD5?

      The Equinix NY4 and LD5 IBX Data Centers in New York and London host over 60 exchanges and trading platforms, 400+ buy and sell side firms, 150+ financial service providers, and offer 675+ network bandwidth options. These data centers are renowned as the most reliable in the world. Within these facilities, financial market participants can interact with each other with minimal latency, ensuring ultra-fast data transfer and leading to the fastest execution speeds for the end user.

    • The Equinix NY4 Advantage

      The Vortex FX MetaTrader 5 servers are presently housed in the NY4 data center in New York and are colocated with our price providers, including an aggregation of 50 banks and dark pool liquidity venues.

      Hosting the MetaTrader 5 servers in NY4 facilitates cross connects between the MetaTrader server and all of Vortex FX's price providers. These dedicated lines offer the lowest latency possible, ensuring the fastest execution speeds for clients.

      The rapid execution speeds attained through our low latency connection provide optimal trading conditions for traders worldwide. These conditions guarantee that orders are filled at the best possible market price.

    • The Equinix LD5 Advantage

      Our cTrader servers are currently situated in the LD5 data center in London and also benefit from collocation with our price providers, similar to the MetaTrader servers.

      The cTrader server was purposefully crafted for rapid connectivity, with no compromises made to ensure the finest possible experience for forex traders. This renders cTrader the optimal platform and setup for both manual and automated traders located in and around Europe.
