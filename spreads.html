cTrader and TradingView
Commission Rates

On the cTrader and TradingView platforms, commission is applied per side, per 100,000 USD traded. This differs from MetaTrader 5, which charges a fixed amount per lot.

The commission rate on the cTrader and TradingView platforms is $3 USD per 100,000 USD traded. The commission amount in USD is converted to the base currency of the account at the current spot rate. An example of the commission calculation on a Trader account is provided below.
Calculation @ $3 USD per 100,000 USD Commission on 1 lot EURUSD
Opening Price €100,000 x 1.3000 = USD $130,000 x (3 / 100,000) $3.90
Closing Price €100,000 x 1.3100 = USD $131,000 x (3 / 100,000) $3.93
Total Commission on Trade $7.83

Low Commission

The commission applies solely to Forex and Metals and varies depending on the trading account type.
