Trading Conditions

The Vortex FX trading environment provides the most aggressive pricing and flexible trading conditions globally. It is ideal for traders who seek the best execution quality and lowest spreads.
Start Trading

  • Spreads

    Our spreads are market-leading, with prices streaming from over 25 different sources of pricing at the highest speed. This ensures that you will always be trading at the best available price. Spreads can be as low as 0.0 pips on our MetaTrader 5 Raw Platforms.

  • Execution

    Our average execution speed is under 40 milliseconds once the order is received by Vortex FX, making it one of the fastest in the industry. Gain access to unparalleled depth of market with one of the most trusted Forex CFD providers, supported by ultra-fast servers in New York and London.

  • Margin and Leverage

    By providing leverage up to 1:500, we facilitate access to the forex market for retail traders. Utilizing leverage requires only a small initial outlay (margin), allowing traders to amplify gains. However, it's important to note that losses can also exceed your initial deposit when trading with leverage.

  • Currency Pairs

    Vortex FX streams prices on 60 different currency pairs and 4 metals across all trading platforms. By aggregating quotes from over 25 different pricing sources, Vortex FX is able to offer some of the best pricing in the industry. This, combined with unrivaled execution speeds, makes Vortex FX the preferred destination for day traders, scalpers, and those using Expert Advisors (EAs).

  • Swap Rates

    We provide both long and short swap rates for all our currency pairs and metals. Swap rates are determined by the overnight interest rate differential between the two currencies involved in the pair and whether the position is a buy (long) or sell (short).

  • Trading Hours

    We make it easy for you to know when the markets are open for trading. You can easily view all trading hours for our broad range of products at a glance. Additionally, trading hours can be viewed directly from within the MetaTrader, cTrader, and TradingView trading terminals.
