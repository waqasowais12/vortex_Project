  • Fast order execution

    The Vortex FX MetaTrader 5 server is strategically located in the Equinix NY4 data center in New York. This financial ecosystem hosts over 600 buy and sell side firms, exchanges, trading venues, and market data and service providers. Our MetaTrader servers are cross-connected to our Communication Network, ensuring minimal latency and lightning-fast execution of your trades within Vortex FX trading environment.

    With latency of less than 1 millisecond to major VPS providers either collocated in the NY4 data center or through dedicated lines to nearby data centers, our trading servers create an ideal environment for automated trading, High-Frequency Trading, and scalping strategies.

  • Spreads from 0.0 pips

    Vortex FX offers some of the tightest spreads among forex brokers worldwide, starting at 0.0 pips on the MetaTrader 5 platforms, with an average of 0.1 pips on EURUSD 24/5. This makes it one of the tightest average EURUSD spreads globally.

    Our pricing connector aggregates a mix from up to 25 different price providers, ensuring we always source the best price for our clients and maintain tight spreads, particularly during high volatility periods such as news announcements.

  • No Restrictions onTrading – Scalping Allowed

    Vortex FX MetaTrader 5 platforms offer unrestricted trading, making them ideal for scalping and high-frequency trading strategies. There are no minimum order distance restrictions, and the freeze level is set to 0, allowing traders to place orders, including stop-loss orders, as close to the market price as desired.

    Furthermore, traders can hedge positions without being subject to the first in, first out (FIFO) rule with Vortex FX. It's essential to note that different hedge margin levels and settings may apply to various products and platforms.

  • Level II Pricing – MarketDepth

    The Depth of Market feature provides traders with a comprehensive view of executable prices sourced directly from our liquidity providers. It offers complete transparency of the liquidity of each currency pair by displaying the available volumes for each price level at any given time. With high liquidity, asynchronous spot prices, and low latency, our Depth of Market ensures the tightest possible spreads for traders.

  • Flexible Funding and
    Withdrawal Options

    Once your account is opened, you can fund it using any of our convenient funding options, including credit/debit card, Skrill, wire transfer, Neteller, FasaPay, China Union Pay, Bpay, and broker-to-broker transfer deposits.

  • Flexible Lot Sizing

    There are no limits or restrictions on trade sizes with Vortex FX. You can place trades as small as one micro lot (0.01). Our flexible lot sizing enables you to trial the platform with minimal risk and manage your trade sizes in line with your account balance.

  • Forex, CFDs on Commodities and Global Indices

    With Vortex FX, you can trade 60 currency pairs, 16 CFDs on commodities (including metals), and 16 major indices (such as the FTSE 100, S&P/ASX 200, and Dow Jones Index) 24 hours a day. Enjoy spreads as low as 0.5 points, providing you with competitive pricing and ample trading opportunities across various asset classes.

  • Leverage up to 1:500

    Vortex FX offers leverage of up to 1:500 on the MetaTrader 5 platforms. Traders can utilize higher leverage to tailor it to their trading style and optimize their manual and automated trading strategies for enhanced flexibility and potential returns.

  • All Major Account Currencies Supported

    We understand that some traders prefer to operate in their local currency. That's why we offer the option to open an account in one of 10 supported base currencies: USD, AUD, EUR, GBP, SGD, NZD, JPY, CHF, HKD, and CAD.
